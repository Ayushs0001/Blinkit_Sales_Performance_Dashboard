### 1. 🛒 Blinkit Sales Performance Dashboard: Unlocking Insights from India's Last-Minute Delivery App

An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze Blinkit's sales performance, outlet characteristics, customer ratings, and product distribution across various outlet types, sizes, and locations.

---

## 2. Short Description

The Blinkit Sales Performance Dashboard provides a comprehensive view of sales trends, product performance, outlet efficiency, and customer satisfaction metrics. It enables stakeholders to identify growth opportunities, optimize inventory strategies, and improve operational performance through data-driven insights.

---

## 3. Tech Stack

The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:

* 📊 **Power BI Desktop** – Primary platform for dashboard creation and visualization.
* 📂 **Power Query** – Used for data cleaning, transformation, and preprocessing.
* 🧠 **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – Developed KPIs, calculated measures, and dynamic analytics.
* 🔗 **Data Modeling** – Structured sales and outlet data for efficient reporting.
* 📈 **Interactive Visualizations** – KPI Cards, Area Charts, Donut Charts, Bar Charts, Tables, and Slicers.
* 📁 **File Formats** – `.pbix`, `.pbit`, `.xlsx`, and dashboard screenshots.

---

## 4. Data Source

### Source: Blinkit Grocery Sales Dataset

The dataset contains transactional and outlet-level information, including:

* Product Categories
* Fat Content
* Item Types
* Outlet Size
* Outlet Type
* Outlet Location Tier
* Sales Revenue
* Customer Ratings
* Item Visibility
* Outlet Establishment Year

The data was transformed and modeled using Power Query before visualization.

---

## 5. Features / Highlights

### • Business Problem

Blinkit operates across multiple outlet formats and locations while managing thousands of grocery products. Understanding sales performance, customer preferences, and outlet effectiveness can be challenging when working with raw operational data.

Key questions include:

* Which outlet types generate the highest revenue?
* How does outlet size impact sales performance?
* Which product categories contribute the most sales?
* What is the relationship between outlet location and revenue generation?
* How do customer ratings vary across outlet formats?

---

### • Goal of the Dashboard

The dashboard aims to:

* Monitor overall business performance through key KPIs.
* Analyze sales across outlet types, sizes, and locations.
* Identify high-performing product categories.
* Evaluate customer satisfaction using ratings.
* Support inventory planning and strategic decision-making.

---

### • Walkthrough of Key Visuals

#### KPI Overview Cards

The dashboard provides a quick summary of business performance through four key metrics:

* **Total Sales:** $1.20M
* **Average Sales:** $141
* **Number of Items:** 8,523
* **Average Rating:** 3.9

These KPIs help management quickly assess overall business health.

---

#### Outlet Establishment Trend

An area chart visualizes sales performance based on outlet establishment years.

**Key Insight:**
Sales peaked around 2018 with approximately $205K, indicating stronger performance among outlets established during that period.

**Business Value:**
Helps evaluate long-term outlet performance and expansion effectiveness.

---

#### Fat Content Analysis

A donut chart compares sales contribution from:

* Low Fat Products
* Regular Products

**Key Insight:**
Regular-fat products contribute a larger share of total sales compared to low-fat alternatives.

**Business Value:**
Supports product assortment and inventory planning decisions.

---

#### Sales by Item Type

A horizontal bar chart ranks product categories by sales contribution.

Top-performing categories include:

* Fruits & Vegetables
* Snack Foods
* Household Items
* Frozen Foods
* Dairy Products

**Business Value:**
Identifies categories driving revenue and supports stock optimization.

---

#### Fat Content by Outlet

A segmented bar chart compares low-fat and regular product sales across outlet tiers.

**Business Value:**
Reveals consumer preferences by location and outlet category.

---

#### Outlet Size Analysis

A donut chart analyzes sales contribution by outlet size:

* Medium
* Small
* High

**Key Insight:**
Medium-sized outlets generate the highest sales contribution.

**Business Value:**
Helps management determine the most effective outlet format for expansion.

---

#### Outlet Location Analysis

A funnel/bar chart compares sales performance across location tiers:

* Tier 1
* Tier 2
* Tier 3

**Key Insight:**
Tier 3 locations contribute the highest revenue among all outlet tiers.

**Business Value:**
Assists in market expansion and regional investment decisions.

---

#### Outlet Type Performance Matrix

A detailed performance table compares outlet types based on:

* Total Sales
* Number of Items
* Average Sales
* Average Rating
* Item Visibility

**Key Findings:**

* Grocery Stores generate the highest sales among outlet categories.
* Supermarket Type 3 maintains strong sales with high inventory availability.
* Ratings remain relatively consistent across outlet formats.

**Business Value:**
Supports benchmarking and operational performance evaluation.

---
